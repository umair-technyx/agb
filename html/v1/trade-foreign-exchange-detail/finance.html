Fixed Assets and Equipment Financing

Finance the purchase of fixed assets to growth your busines. You may now finance up to 75% of you equipment’s value.

Once you have passed the eligibility criteria, you are then entitled to avail an array of benefits provided to you by Africa and Gulf Bank.

- Transparency is essential; Terms and conditions, as well as eligibility criteria, are simple and clear.

- Financing available up to 75% of the Equipment/Machinery Quotation Value.

- Financing plans are valid for up to 48 months.

- Repayable in similar monthly installments.

- Fewer paperwork and quicker clearance.

- Interest rates are the lowest in the economy.

- Low transaction fees.

- Fast decision-making and processing turnaround times, with the majority of credit decisions made within 48 hours.
