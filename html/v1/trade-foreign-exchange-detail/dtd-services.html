Corporate Multi Currency Account

With your Corporate Multi-Currency Account, leave all your currency worries behind. With a choice of widely used currencies, managing your foreign exchange is now without hassle.

This account is both Efficient and cost-effective. It does not only help you to transact across borders seamlessly but also save on administrative costs with just one account for multiple wallets carrying different currencies.

Additionally, you’ll be pleased to know that an account can be opened with a minimum of only $3,000 or equivalent.
